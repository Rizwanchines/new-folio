About ME

A bit about
who I am

I'm Rizwan, a freelance designer and frontend developer based in Paris with 5 years of experience shipping digital products for startups, agencies, and scale-ups across Europe. I thrive at the intersection of great design and clean code.

I believe great interfaces are invisible — they get out of the user's way. My work is fast, accessible and built to last. When I'm not coding, you'll find me hiking or hunting for a good espresso.
